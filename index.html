About PERGAM Victoria

PERGAM Victoria is committed to supporting the Malay community in Victoria on their Qur’anic learning journey. Our mission is to cultivate Mu’min who read, understand, and practice the Qur’an in daily life.

We offer programs, courses, and resources designed to connect students with experienced Qur’an teachers, encourage lifelong learning, and foster a strong culture of Qur’anic education within the home.

Our Objectives

Fostering a culture of lifelong Qur'anic learning and knowledge sharing among educators and the broader Victorian Malay community through connection with Qur'an teachers.

  • Provide access to Malay Qur’an teachers in Victoria.
  • Promote daily Qur’anic reading habits at home.
  • Improve teachers' knowledge and skills.
  • Organize Qur’anic competitions and motivational programs.
  • Support teachers’ well-being and development.
  • Become a reference for Qur’an learning and understanding.

Logo Symbolism
